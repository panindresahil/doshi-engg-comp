about us

We introduce ourselves as one of the leading Fabricators & Exporters of Chemical Skid mounted Plants and process equipments.

Viz: REACTION VESSEL, PRESSURE VESSELS, HEAT EXCHANGERS, CONDENSERS, DISTILLATION COLUMNS AND STILLS, STORAGE TANKS, AUTOCLAVES, BLENDERS, MEASURING VESSELS, ETC.SINCE 1960.

We have made critical equipments for various Clients. We have fabrication facilities with our group companies with 100 workmen and with handling facility of 10 tons to 50 M.T.E.O.T. crane and large open area. We have made vessels weighing up to 50 M.T.

We are approved for fabrication of Unfired Pressure Vessels under SMPV (U) Rule 1981.
